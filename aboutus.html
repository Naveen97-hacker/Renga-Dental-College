About Us

Renga Dental was founded by Dr. Srinidhi Dwaraka and Vishaal Rengan in 2015 in two hundred and forty square feet on a quiet road in Chennai. Today, the small clinic has metamorphosed into Renga Dental. In this span of nearly half a decade, we have gained the trust of patients who are happy to come back and bring along with them their friends and family.

Renga Dental is housed in fourteen thousand square feet in centrally located Nungambakkam with sixteen well appointed dental operatories in a sparklingly clean, pleasant and hygienic environment. A team of dedicated specialists and general dentists welcome patients to the ideal blend of western technology and eastern skills.
